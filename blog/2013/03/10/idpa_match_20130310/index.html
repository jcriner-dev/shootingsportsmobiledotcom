First Live Fire Match

10 Mar 2013

After a considerable amount of testing, we held our first IDPA ™ match of the year. Signup went relatively smoothly other than a few registrations that were re-runs and needed to be fixed after the match. Electronic scoring from the tablets was backed up on paper and there were only a few inconsistencies that needed to be fixed. Scores were corrected and completed within an hour after the match was over. Results were emailed out before we left the club. We had eight (8) squads / on eight (8) stages, things went better than expected, and there were a lot of smiling faces.
