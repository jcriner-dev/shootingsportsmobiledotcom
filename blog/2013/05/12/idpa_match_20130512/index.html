May IDPA Match

12 May 2013

Things are moving along at our local club. Everyone knows how to handle the signup process. Three (3) late shooters were added to squads on the bays after the mobile devices were deployed. It's not as quick as squadding from the signup list, but it works just fine.

Seven (7) more squads on eight (8) stages, everyone is getting the hang of it, and no one wants to go back to paper!
