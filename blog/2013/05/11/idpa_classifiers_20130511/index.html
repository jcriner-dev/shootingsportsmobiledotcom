May IDPA Classifiers

11 May 2013

Today we ran our first group of classifiers at the local club and things went very well. You can sign up classifiers on the mobile device at the bay pretty quickly. Once you save the proper number stages with the proper number of string times for each stage, the SO gets an alert indicating the total classifier score and how the shooter classified.

After uploading the results to the main computer, you can generate a classifier report for the day and export a proper csv file to upload the classifiers to IDPA HQ ™.
