May, June, July IDPA

12 Jul 2015

The May, June, and July matches went off without a hitch.

The only changes to the software were updates to how you login at IDPA HQ, some font size changes, and updated messages when you sign up someone who has already shot the match.

We are also making great progress training new scorekeepers and now have a lot of personnel redundancy. With three to four (3 - 4) people available to sign shooters up, some of us can move on to other pre-match tasks. Many thanks to all of our new scorekeepers for their efforts.
