April IDPA

12 Apr 2015

Singup was a little delayed for a couple of reasons. A) we were training new scorekeepers; and B) we had 104 shooters (including 20 new shooters) for the first match of the year! Nothing to complain about. We brought in a second computer and an iPhone 6+ to help with signup and that did the trick.

Scoring went smoothly out on the range and there were no questions or concerns raised by the SO's. We are now on year three (3) and everyone seems to have the hang of things.

Results went out as usual, with the addition of a "results by stage" report.
