IDPA 2022 10th Anniversary

09 Oct 2022

Another season is complete at our local club. The scoring system has again proven itself to be two things: 1) simple and easy to use; and 2) utterly reliable.

The scoring software started as an idea in November 2012. Alphabetizing and adding scoresheets was getting old and Practiscore wasn't quite ready for IDPA. Six (6) months later we ran our first match fully electronically, and never looked back.

76 matches, 319 classifiers, and 50422 stages have been scored under live fire conditions, including the 2022 IDPA Michigan State Match (Tier 3).

It has been an amazing 10 years.

Thanks to everyone involved.
