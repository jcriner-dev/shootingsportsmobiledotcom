IDPA 2022 Michigan State Match (Tier 3)

14 Aug 2022

It was a bit of a wet one on Saturday, but Ziploc bags got us through. There were a few minor human errors due to raind rops on screens that were easily corrected. Other than that, the scoring system worked as expected for a major match.

The protest period started within 10 minutes of the last shot being fired. The results were emailed out, uploaded to IDPA, and all bumps were applied at IDPA while the awards were still being handed out.

Thanks to everyone who made the scoring process run smoothly and the match in general a success.
