May IDPA Match

11 May 2014

Seven (7) squads on eight (8) stages.

Things keep chugging along without many issues. We are now up to 11 events, 83 classifiers, 787 participants, 6463 stages scored under live fire conditions.

There was one accidental DQ due to operator error. We have added a more substantial alert system which should help prevent this issue from happening in the future. We also finally encountered the "what if the electronic device fails" issue. In this instance we swapped tablets, re-squadded, and carried on. We were also able to recover the data from the "malfunctioning" tablet and upload it to the database. So, in the end, nothing was lost.
