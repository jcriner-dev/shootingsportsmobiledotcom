Battle Tested

13 Oct 2014

During the two (2) years of scoring with Shooting Sports Mobile at our local club, we have now scored 18 matches, 113 classifiers, and 9811 stages under live fire conditions.

Half of our SO's took one (1) thirty (30) minute training class. Everyone else learned on the fly during a match.

We backed up the scoring system with paper... once. After that shooters were encouraged to keep their own score if they felt a backup was necessary.

We have had one (1) tablet go down during a match in two years. We swapped to a backup tablet, re-squadded, and were back up and running in under five (5) minutes. Data from the troubled tablet was recovered back at the clubhouse and merged with the rest of the results.

Easy to use. High reliability. Results with the push of a button.
