October IDPA

12 Oct 2014

For the last match of the season, we decided to cater to BUG guns. On Sunday we ran three (3) matches simultaneously, the Main Match, a BUG Pistol Match, and a BUG Revolver Match all with eight (8) stages. Signup went as smooth as ever, and we had 59 shooters in the Main Match, 12 shooters in BUG Pistol, and 7 shooters in BUG Revolver.

Scoring was straight forward as the scoring program allows you to have shooters from different matches on the same squad. Results were done for all three matches and season standings within the hour. Then it was on to the awards and banquet.

Thanks again to everyone for another great season and all your support!
